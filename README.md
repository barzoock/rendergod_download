## What is RenderGod?

**[RenderGod](https://rendergod.app/)** is an intelligent Blender render management tool that automates your entire rendering pipeline. It features **automatic crash recovery**, a powerful **render queue**, **real-time Telegram remote control**, **performance optimization**, and **smart export management** — all running 100% locally on your machine.

Stop babysitting your renders. RenderGod handles crashes, freezes, and reboots automatically so your projects finish on time — even while you sleep.
